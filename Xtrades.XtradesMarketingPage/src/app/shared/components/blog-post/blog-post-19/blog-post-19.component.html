A First Of It's Kind Application For Traders: The Xtrades App
01.12.2022

Have you ever thought of a trading app that encompassed everything you needed as a trader? From receiving isolated push notifications, to finding the right mentor and coach that you can strictly follow and listen to for advice, to knowing what the general public thinks about an asset. What about getting educated or using the same tools as the traders that you’re interested in following - this is all possible through downloading our Apple Store Xtrades App, or if you’re an android uer, you can download our Xtrades Android App.

Studies have shown that individuals who teach are able to retain 95% of what they learn. The most enforcing way to learn something is by teaching it to others, and that is exactly what our Xtrades IOS app and Play Store Xtrades app allows us to do. We give people the ability to share their trades in absolute real time and these stats are then crunched into viewable parameters which allow us to understand where we are weak, where we are strong - what types of trades we excel in, what our win rate is in Stocks vs Options, and the list goes on. Our goal is to build proper social infrastructure for Traders and Investors that range from complete beginners to intermediate to advanced. No matter where you are on the spectrum, there is a place for you to contribute, earn, and continue learning from others.

Right now, our Xtrades android app is not as polished as the Iphone version of the Xtrades IOS app but our priorities will be there to ensure that they are equally working just as well. Right now, users can filter their favorite traders out and get alerts from the people that they deem to be the most effective. Previously, it was troublesome to try and filter out notifications via Discord, but now you can operate the app in a way where you can literally only get push notifications from one person if you wanted to. Moving forward we will ensure that the Xtrades IOS app and the Xtrades Android app are equally working - if you have any comments, suggestions, or feature requests feel free to shoot me an email: kevin@xtrades.net

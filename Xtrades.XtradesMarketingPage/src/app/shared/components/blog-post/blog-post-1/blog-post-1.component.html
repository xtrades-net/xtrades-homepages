How Does The Commission-Free Trading Work At Webull, And What Are Some Restrictions Investors Should Be Aware Of Before Signing Up For An Account With Them?

Webull offers commission-free trading on all U.S. exchanges, a great perk for active investors. However, there are a few restrictions that investors should be aware of before signing up for an account with them. For example, only limit and market orders are available for commission-free trading. Additionally, only the following order types are available: regular, stop loss, trailing stop loss, and OCO (one cancels the other). Also, it's important to note that while Webull does not charge any commissions on trades, they do make money from "payment for order flow." When you place a trade through Webull, they will sell your order to another party who will execute the trade on your behalf. While this doesn't cost you anything directly, it does mean that you're not getting the best possible price for your trade.
